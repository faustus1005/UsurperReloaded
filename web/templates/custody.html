Child Care

Manage custody of your children. Be careful - your decisions affect their lives!

Help

Share custody - When you divorce, only one parent gets access. By sharing custody you can again share the responsibility and reap the rewards of parenthood.

Abandon child - You no longer have responsibility for the child. You don't pay for upbringing, but lose experience from parenting. The other parent gets full custody.

Orphanage - Send the child to the Royal Orphanage. You lose all responsibility. Things can get ugly if the Royals can't afford to raise the child.
